Wildfire Prediction

During the last few years, terrible wildfires are spreading all over the world. When the summer season starts, these events tend to appear more often, and the damage and destruction to human lives and property is catastrophic. We decided to build a web-app using AI and ML to help notify people who might be in potential danger of getting affected by these wildfires

Impact on Animals
Due to Wildfires!

Each year an unreported and inconceivable number of foxes, rabbits, deer, frogs, mice, coyotes, and other animals are smoked out of their homes and burned alive.

People are unavoidably separated from their pets and livestock and wildlife often has nowhere to turn.

Wildfires destroy thousands
of structures each year.

Since 2005, more than 89,000 structures have been destroyed by wildfires resulting in an untold number of fatalities, evacuations, and personal losses.

The number of structures destroyed, rather than the number of acres burned, is a more telling measure of the broad social, economic, and community impacts from wildfires.
